## üß† Project Overview
The **AI Prescription Agent** project was developed to automate the process of reading and interpreting handwritten medical prescriptions. It leverages **Optical Character Recognition (OCR)**, **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, and **AI-driven automation** to convert prescriptions into structured digital data that can be logged, analyzed, and shared efficiently.

The project also integrates with **Google Sheets** for secure data storage and **Telegram** for instant prescription summaries and notifications.
